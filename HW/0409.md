## 2
- SARSA: on-policy, both sampling(choosing $a$ in $s$) and target(choosing $a'$ in $s'$) are the same $\epsilon$-greedy policy
- Q-learning: off-policy, sampling(choosing $a$ in $s$) can be any method, but target(for choosing $a'$ in $s'$) is pure greedy($\arg\max$)
